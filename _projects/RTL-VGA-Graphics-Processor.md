## Project Overview

- **HDL coding**: SystemVerilog
- **Protocol**: VGA-ADC
- **Frequency -VGA clock**: 25.175 MHz
- **Pixel resolution {R,G,B}**: 24 bits 
- **LUT resource**: 3488
- **FF count**: 3029
- **Static Power Consumption**: 108 mW
- **Dynamic Power Consumption**: 216 mw
